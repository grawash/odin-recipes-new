Khachapuri

Description:

Khachapuri is traditional georgian dish that is made with cheese and bread and has many varieties from different regions of georgia.

  • 300 g All-purpose flour
  • 150 ml Water (warm)
  • 10 g Dry yeast
  • 500 g Cheese, grated
  • 2 Egg yolks
  • 20 g Butter
  • 1 tsp Sunflower oil, for greasing
  • 1 tsp Salt

Steps:

  1. In a bowl combine warm (not hot) water and dry yeast, stir well and set aside for about 15 minutes.
  2. Add the flour little by little and start to knead the dough.
  3. Continue to knead on the flowered board until the dough is soft.
  4. Put the dough in a bowl, sprinkle with sunflower oil, cover with plastic wrap and let rest for about 40 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven on 220 CÂ°. Divide the dough into 2 balls.
  6. Roll out each dough ball in an oval shape. Seal the longer sides together so that the dough takes a boat shape.
  7. Fill each boat with cheese. If the dough ball is 200 g, cheese filling must be 200 g too.
  8. Bake the khachapuri in the preheated oven, about 12-15 minutes, until golden.
  9. Then take it out, place the egg yolk in the center and continue to bake for another 1-2 minutes. Add butter before serving.
