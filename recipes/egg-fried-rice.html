Egg Fried Rice

Description:

Egg fried rice is a flavorful and easy-to-make dish that combines cooked rice with scrambled eggs, vegetables, and seasonings. Stir-fried in a hot wok or pan, it’s typically flavored with soy sauce, garlic, and green onions. This versatile dish is often enjoyed on its own or as a side, making it a perfect meal for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup sliced green beans or peas
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in instant rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Sauté onions and green beans in hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  4. Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well. Season with pepper.
